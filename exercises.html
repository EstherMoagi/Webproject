Exercises

  • Aerobic exercise-reduces your risk of heart disease.
  • Walking- it burns calories, helps manage blood sugar, is good for your heart and helps preserve your muscle.
  • Running-helps build strong bones and it strengthen muscles,
  • Push-ups- it increase upper body strength.
  • Deadlifts- it builds strength.
  • Lunges-improves your balance and flexibility.
  • Burpees -improves cardiovascular.
  • Cycling- also improves your cardiovascular health.
